About Us

IET-VIT, in collaboration with The Skillship Foundation, will be organizing a summer program for their members to learn new skills, pique new interests and build constructive projects which lead to their holistic development. The participants of the program will be provided with options to choose from any of the following fields :

  • Data Science.
  • Full-Stack.
  • Hardware.

Following the candidateâ€™s choice of domain, he or she will undergo a one month period of training, where mentors from IET-VIT will be guiding them through the process of learning the topics concerning that particular domain, in accordance with our curriculum. Following the one month of training, candidates will be given another month to use the skills they have recently acquired and build a capstone project, which they will then have to submit.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Do I need to have any prior projects to be accepted into this program?

    The selection criteria will be based on resume and prior experience.

  • Am I eligible for this program if Iâ€™m not part of IET?

    If you are a college student you are most welcome to apply for the internship program.

  • What will be the evaluation framework for the internship?

    There will be 4 graded quizzes, and 4 graded on-call reviews, for the first 30 days of the program. In addition, you will be tasked with creating a capstone project in the last 30 days of the program, under the guidance of a mentor.

  • Can I select multiple domains?

    We generally do not encourage the selection of multiple domains as this may affect your performance keeping in consideration the workload of this program.

  • Where will the activities of the Virtual program take place?

    Once the program commences, we will be adding all participants to a google classroom based on their domain, which will be the platform for all discussions.

  • Will I be getting a certificate after I complete the program?

    Yes, you will be eligible for the certificate from IET and affiliated under Microsoft and Skillship Society provided that you complete your capstone project and clear all quizzes and reviews with a minimum score.
